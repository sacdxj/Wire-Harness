About PH Series Connectors

The PH Series is a compact 2.0mm pitch wire-to-board connector system designed for high-density printed circuit board applications. With its friction lock mechanism and space-saving design, the PH Series is ideal for consumer electronics, portable devices, and anywhere board real estate is at a premium.

Our PH Series connectors are fully compatible with JST PH Series connectors, offering a cost-effective alternative without compromising on quality or reliability. All products are manufactured to strict quality standards and undergo rigorous testing to ensure consistent performance.

Key Features

  • Ultra-compact 2.0mm pitch for high-density designs
  • Friction lock mechanism for secure connections
  • Available in 2 to 15 position configurations
  • PA66 GF housing material with UL94V-0 flame rating
  • 100V AC/DC, 2A current rating
  • Operating temperature: -25°C to +85°C
  • Compatible with industry-standard JST PH Series
  • 30 minimum mating cycles
