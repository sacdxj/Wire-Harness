Compact 2.0mm Pitch Design

Ultra-compact footprint ideal for high-density PCB layouts and space-constrained applications in consumer electronics and portable devices.

Friction Lock Mechanism

Secure friction-lock design ensures reliable connection retention without requiring additional locking mechanisms, simplifying assembly.

Premium PA66 GF Material

High-quality glass-filled polyamide housing provides excellent mechanical strength, heat resistance, and chemical resistance for demanding environments.

JST PH Series Compatible

Fully compatible with JST PH Series connectors, offering a cost-effective alternative without compromising quality or performance.
